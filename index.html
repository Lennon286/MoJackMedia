Day 4: Crossing the Glenfinnan Viaduct

Published: 6 May 2025

Today’s challenge was a stunning one. We made our way to the famous Glenfinnan Viaduct — yes, the one from Harry Potter — and decided to walk the full length of the route beside it, despite the relentless wind.

The weather wasn't on our side, but the view more than made up for it. Rolling hills, the echo of a train in the distance, and Dad’s constant grumbling about the incline made it a walk to remember.

Halfway through, we stopped on the ridge. Dad pulled out a flask of tea like a true adventurer, and we sat down to soak in the moment. This wasn’t the hardest challenge so far — but it was easily the most beautiful.
