Day 1: The Pizza Hut Marathon

Published: 4 May 2025

We kicked things off with a challenge that tested both our stomachs and our stamina — the infamous Pizza Hut Marathon. Dad had to run between three different Pizza Hut branches in the city, stopping at each for a single slice. Easy? Think again.

Between traffic, unexpected rain, and a very grumpy delivery driver, it turned into an unforgettable launch to our 50 Fastlane mission.
